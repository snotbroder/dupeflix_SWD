Security Policy

We appreciate responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities in Dupeflix.

How to report

If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us via email before making it public.

What we expect

  • Give us reasonable time to fix the vulnerability before disclosure
  • Avoid accessing or modifying other users' data
  • Act in good faith

What you can expect from us

  • We will confirm receipt of your report within 7 days
  • We will keep you updated on the status of the fix
  • We will credit you in our acknowledgments if you wish
